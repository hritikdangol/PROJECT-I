Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about SaveBites? Find the answers below.

What is SaveBites?

SaveBites is a platform that connects local restaurants, volunteers, and communities to reduce food waste. Restaurants can list their leftover food so it can reach nearby customers or be distributed to those in need.

How can my restaurant join SaveBites?

You can click on the "Register your Restaurant" button on our homepage, fill out the basic details about your business, and our team will verify your account within 24 hours.

Is the listed food safe to consume?

Yes. All partner restaurants must follow standard food safety and hygiene regulations. The food listed consists of fresh leftovers from the day that haven't been served.

How can I help as a volunteer?

Volunteers help by picking up food from restaurants and delivering it to local shelters or community centers. Go to our "Volunteer" page to sign up!

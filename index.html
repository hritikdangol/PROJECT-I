About SaveBites

Food waste is one of the biggest challenges affecting our environment and communities. SaveBites provides a simple solution by connecting restaurants with customers and volunteers to give surplus food a meaningful purpose.

Through our platform, restaurants can share their extra food, customers can enjoy affordable meals, and volunteers can help deliver support where it is needed most. We believe every meal has value, and by working together, we can reduce waste, protect the environment, and create a more caring community.

SaveBites is more than a food-sharing platform — it is a movement toward sustainability, responsibility, and a future where food is respected and nothing valuable is wasted.
